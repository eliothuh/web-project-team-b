    About The Data

    What It Consists Of

    Our dataset reports homicide statistics for counties and states in the U.S. It covers the number of homicides annually from 1999 to 2017 and the means of homicide.

    Data is grouped by region and cause of death.

    Where It Is From

    The data is from The U.S.Center for Disease Control and Prevention WONDER database, a reliable government-run public database.

    How we Processed Our data

    The data was downloaded as plain text (.txt) files and converted to comma-separated value (.csv) files. Then, data values marked "unreliable" were replaced with null values for formatting purposes.
