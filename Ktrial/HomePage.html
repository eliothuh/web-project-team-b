About The Data

What It Consists Of

Our dataset reports homicide statistics for counties and states of the United States.

Our data summarizes, processes, and categorizes all officially recorded cases of homicides from the years of 1999 to 2017.

The data is grouped by region, time, and underlying cause.

Where It Is From

The data is from The U.S.Center for Disease Control and Prevention WONDER database, a reliable government-run database.

How we Processed Our data

The data was downloaded as plain text (.txt) files and converted to comma-separated value (.csv) files. Then, data values marked "unreliable" were replaced with null values for formatting purposes.
